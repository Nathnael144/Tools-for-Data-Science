     Analytic Approach

Analytic Approach Selection
- The choice of analytic approach depends on the specific question being asked and requires clarification from the stakeholder.
- Understanding the question allows for the identification of the necessary patterns to address it effectively.

Types of Analytic Models
- Predictive models are used for determining probabilities of actions, while descriptive approaches analyze relationships and clusters of activities.
- Classification models are suitable for yes/no questions, providing a clear prediction based on statistical analysis.

Case Study: Decision Tree Classification
- A decision tree model was applied to identify conditions leading to patient outcomes, offering insights into readmission risks.
- This model is user-friendly for clinicians, allowing them to understand high-risk factors and adapt treatment strategies accordingly.
